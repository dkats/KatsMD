Massachusetts Smokers' Helpline: 1-800-QUIT-NOW
Call 7 days per week/24 hours per day (holiday hours may vary) to receive counseling online, by phone or through eChat and eight weeks of free nicotine patches, lozenges or gum

My Life, My Quit: reach trained youth coach using live text messaging or online chat. Call or text "Start My Quit" to 855-891-9989 for free and confidential help. To sign up online, visit mylifemyquit.com.
