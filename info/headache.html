  • Eat breakfast and lunch every day; protein shakes are ok
  • Consider a trial of MigreLief (riboflavin (vitamin B2), Puracol Feverfew, magnesium)

  • For headache - trial of Excedrin with caffeine (migraine preparation) as soon as headache starts.

Headache Diary

Keeping a headache diary can help your doctor make an accurate diagnosis and develop an effective treatment plan. For each headache, write down the following:

  • Date
  • Time
  • Location on head
  • Throbbing?
  • Intensity (1-10)
  • What were you doing when the headache started?
  • Nausea/Vomiting?
  • What helped the headache go away? (e.g. sleep, quiet, Tylenol, ibuprofen)
  • Duration
