Asthma

  • Allergy

If child needs 2 or more doses a week of albuterol or multiple times in one day (not counting doses used before exercise to prevent exercise-induced asthma) or has daytime symptoms 2 or more days a week or awakens from asthma more than 1 day a month or has any limitation of activity level and exercise ability due to asthma or is unable to be fully active, please set up an appointment with your pediatrician to adjust the medications used to control the asthma.
