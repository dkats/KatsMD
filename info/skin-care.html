Moisturizers

The best moisturizers are the ones that comes in a tub. The grosser/greasier, the better, because it will trap in more moisture. Creams and ointments are good to use, but lotions won't work well.

Creams/ointments: Fragrance-free is best, such as plain petroleum jelly, Vaseline, Aquaphor, Vanicream, Eucerin, CeraVe, Cetaphil Restoraderm, Aveeno Eczema Therapy, and California Baby Calming.

Body wash: Fragrance-free moisturizing bars or body washes are best, such as Purpose, Cetaphil, Dove sensitive skin, Aveeno, California Baby, or Vanicream.

Detergent: Consider using fragrance- and dye-free detergent, such as Arm and Hammer for sensitive skin, Tide Free, or All Free.
