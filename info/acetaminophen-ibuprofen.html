Acetaminophen (Tylenol) and Advil/Motrin (Ibuprofen) Dosing

The appropriate dose of acetaminophen (Tylenol) and Advil/Motrin (ibuprofen) changes based on the child's weight. Here are chart to help you figure out the appropriate dose. Please note, these are only accurate if the liver and kidneys are healthy. Please ask your doctor for help if you are unsure.
