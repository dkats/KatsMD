Vaping

Resources

This is Quitting: free and confidential texting program for young people who vape. Text “VapeFreeMass” to 88709 to get started.

My Life, My Quit: Call or text "Start My Quit" to 855-891-9989 to connect with a trained coach.

MGH Community Health Associates' Living TOBACCO-FREE: Appointments available in Spanish and English at MGH Health Centers in Revere, Everett, Chelsea, and Charlestown. Call them at 781-485-6210.

MGH Addiction Recovery Management Service: an outpatient, dual diagnosis clinic made up of a multidisciplinary team of clinical psychiatrists, psychologists and masters-level social workers who are trained to work with youth and their parents to provide an individualized plan for recovery
