Clean-out

MiraLAX "Mini" Clean-out Instructions

  1. Start with 2 small rectangles of chocolate Ex-Lax
  2. Mix 17g of MiraLAX into 4-6 ounces of clear liquid (juice, Gatorade, water). Have your child drink this down in 10-25 minutes
  3. Repeat step 2 every 40 minutes, 7 times in a row
  4. If no results by the following day, may repeat steps 1-3 again
  5. If still no results, call our office for further instructions

Maintenance

  • Start after completing the clean-out regimen
  • MiraLAX/polyethylene glycol ___ capfuls (17 grams per capful) in 8oz of liquid by mouth ___ times per day until next visit

The goal of maintenance therapy is to achieve daily soft, comfortable bowel movements that have the consistency of soft-serve ice-cream/mashed potatoes. You may need to adjust the MiraLAX dosing to achieve this consistency. When doing this, make dose changes by ¼ capful (1 teaspoon) at a time and stay with the new dose for a minimum of 3 days before making the next dose change.

Behavior Plan

  • If your child is toilet trained:
    • Sitting plan: sit on toilet for 3-5 min after meals
      • Do something relaxing on the toilet – deep breathing, reading, playing game
      • Try something to help relax the muscles: blow pinwheel
      • Evacuation exercises, such as blowing a balloon, helps practice good pushing while on the toilet
  • For toddlers and school-aged kids
    • Exercise regularly: 30 min of physical activity per day

Diet Plan

  • Limit
    • Limit Carbohydrates such as pasta, rice, bread, bagels, and cereals. When Carbohydrates are part of the diet, choose high fiber or whole grain
    • Avoid refined sugars and starches made with white flour
    • Limit “binding” foods such as milk, cheese and bananas
  • Increase
    • Increase fruits and vegetables for fiber and healthy gut bacteria
    • Eat fruits and vegetables with the peels or skins on
    • Increase essential fatty acids in the diet such as nuts, seeds, beans, fish, avocado and olive oil
    • Increase lean protein source
    • Talk to your doctor about taking probiotics (5-10 billion live organisms daily), multivitamin with minerals, digestive enzymes, magnesium, and B-vitamins
    • Drink plenty of fluids. Set a goal of at least 8 cups per day. Fluid helps your body process fiber without discomfort
