Diaper Rash

Dr. Daniel Doody's Butt Balm

  • 1 part zinc oxide powder
  • 1 part cornstarch
  • 2 parts petroleum jelly
  • 2 parts stomadhesive powder

Use mineral oil or Maalox to clean the skin. Avoid water or diaper wipes, which will cause discomfort to the child and may aggravate the irritation.

Apply paste to the area at least 4 times/day, or if the paste washes off the area.
