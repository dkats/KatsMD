Teeth

When Should We Start Going to the Dentist?

I recommend starting to see the dentist by 3 years of age. Kids still have baby teeth then, but that's a great time to practice dental care before the adult (permanent) teeth come in. Going to the dentist can be uncomfortable, so I recommend starting early to get used to it and developing a trusting relationship with the dentist.
