Medical School Textbooks

These are the textbooks that were recommended to me during medical school. This page is a continuation of my clinical references, which has more clinically relevant resources.

Medicine

  • Medical Physiology – Boron
  • Harrison's Principles of Internal Medicine
  • Costanzo's Physiology

Psychiatry

  • The American Psychiatric Publishing Textbook of Psychiatry
  • DSM
  • Dulcan’s Textbook of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry
  • The APA Textbook of Psychopharmacology
  • Manual of Clinical Psychopharmacology

Cardiology

  • Pathophysiology of Heart Disease (Lilly)
  • Cardiovascular Physiology (Klabunde)

Pulmonology

  • Principles of Pulmonary Medicine
  • Pulmonary Pathophysiology: The Essentials

Gastroenterology

  • Lange Gastrointestinal Physiology
  • Gastrointestinal Physiology

Nephrology

Endocrinology

  • Greenspan’s Basic & Clinical Endocrinology

Neurology

  • Adams and Victor's Principles of Neurology

Ob/gyn

  • *Larsen's Human Embryology – Schoenwolf
  • Comprehensive Gynecology – Lentz
  • Obstetrics: Normal and Problem Pregnancies – Gabbe

Genetics

  • Thompson & Thompson Genetics in Medicine

Hematology/Oncology

  • Holland-Frei Cancer Medicine
  • Pathophysiology of Blood Disorders
  • Hematology: Basic Principles and Practice

Infectious Disease

  • Immunobiology
  • Cellular and Molecular Immunology
  • Review of Medical Microbiology and Immunology
  • Medical Microbiology

Orthopedics

  • Musculoskeletal Medicine for Medical Students

Dermatology

  • Fitzpatrick's Dermatology in General Medicine
  • Andrews' Diseases of the Skin

Rheumatology

  • Kelley and Firestein's Textbook of Rheumatology

Anatomy
