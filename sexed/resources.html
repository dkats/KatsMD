Sexually Transmitted Infections/Diseases (STIs/STDs)

The United States Centers for Disease Control (CDC) have a great website that goes over the latest STI information. Part of it is quick fact sheets on many common STIs.

UpToDate is a great resource for patients, and is basically the medical version of Wikipedia. All of the articles are written by an expert and edited by other experts to ensure high-quality information.

Contraception / Birth Control

One of my favorite resources is this fantastic New York Times article that quickly runs through different contraception options and has some nice interactive graphs. The important thing to remember, though, is this article focuses on preventing pregnancy, not preventing spread of STIs.

There's also a great CDC website with reproductive health and contraception information.

Regardless of your political beliefs, Planned Parenthood has a fantastic website that goes through lots of different contraception options.

Bedsider.org is also a great resource for contraception information.
