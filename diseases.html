The point of these pages is to provide brief info sheets (for patients) with lots of resources from high-quality, reliable sources for more information. It's very difficult to know where to look for answers, so I try to provide some starting places. Obviously, I can't vet each source and am definitely missing many, but I hope to give a good start. I also try to include each disease under each of its synonyms, so some diseases link to the same page from multiple spots below.
