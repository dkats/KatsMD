Amoxicillin-clavulanate (Augmentin) Calculator

Amoxicillin-clavulanate (Augmentin) is an incredibly confusing medication to prescribe, due to the many different formulations. It is very commonly prescribed incorrectly, which leads to inadequate treatment (iatrogenic treatment failure) and poor antibiotic stewardship. This calculator is meant to guide you towards the correct amoxicillin-clavulanate formulation to prescribe.

NOTE: This is not meant to be used in place of a real-life pharmacist. Please always confirm with them if you have doubts or questions about proper amoxicillin-clavulanate formulation selection.
