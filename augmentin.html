Amoxicillin-clavulanate (Augmentin) Calculator

Amoxicillin-clavulanate (Augmentin) is an incredibly confusing medication to prescribe, due to the many different formulations. It is very commonly prescribed incorrectly, which leads to inadequate treatment (iatrogenic treatment failure) and poor antibiotic stewardship. This calculator is meant to guide you towards the correct amoxicillin-clavulanate formulation to prescribe. Just fill out the patient's age, indication, and weight. The calculator will figure out the dosing and only display the formulations with an appropriate amount of clavulanate (unless you change the setting at the bottom to show all the formulations). You can override the dosing and frequency if you want.

NOTE: This is not meant to be used in place of a real-life pharmacist. Please always confirm with them if you have doubts or questions about proper amoxicillin-clavulanate formulation selection.
